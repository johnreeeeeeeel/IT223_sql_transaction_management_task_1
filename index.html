Execute theStoredProcedure:

CALL EmployeeTransaction(1,-75,2);

What happen to the account of employee#1? Why?

The account of employee#1 will be deducted by 75, since the final balance is 400 then we input the amount as -75. So 400-75=325, the the final balance will be 325.

Execute theStoredProcedure:

CALL EmployeeTransaction(2,500,2);

What happen to the account of employee#2? Why?

The account of employee#2 will be added by two 500, since the final balance is 900 then we input the amount as 500 two times. So 900+500+500=1900, the the final balance will be 1900.

What happen to the account of employee#2? Why?

The account of employee#2 will be added by three 500, since the final balance is 1200 then we input the amount as 500 three times. So 1200+500+500+500=2700, the the final balance will be 2700.
